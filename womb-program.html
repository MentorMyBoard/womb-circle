The Context

Why WOMB Exists

India's corporate landscape is witnessing an unprecedented transformation in board composition, governance expectations and diversity mandates. While organizations increasingly seek competent women directors, many experienced professionals lack structured pathways to develop board-specific competencies.

WOMB was conceptualized to bridge this critical leadership gap — creating a rigorous learning ecosystem where accomplished women executives transition into confident, board-ready leaders capable of contributing to strategic oversight, governance excellence and sustainable value creation.
